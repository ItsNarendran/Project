What is this?

This is a free, very simple browser-based online screen recorder.

  • Step 1 - capture your screen with (or without) a voiceover
  • Step 2 - one-click share the recording with a co-worker, friend or a family member

No plugins, no extensions, no 3rd party software required, just a modern browser.

If something's not working: ask your coworker to close their browser, then refresh this page, re-share the screen and send the link to your coworker again.
