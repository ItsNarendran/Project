What is this?

This is a free, very basic browser based screen sharing app. Use it to quickly share your screen with a co-worker.

No plugins, no extensions, no 3rd party software like TeamViewer or something. Just a modern browser that supports WebRTC.

At PeerShare, we understand the value of seamless collaboration. Our platform is designed with simplicity, security, and productivity in mind. Say goodbye to complicated setups and hello to effortless screen sharing. Join us today and experience the future of online collaboration.
